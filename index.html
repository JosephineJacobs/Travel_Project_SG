Must try in Singapore

Hainanese Chicken Rice

Hainanese chicken rice is a beloved dish originating from the Hainan province in southern China but is widely popular in Singapore, Malaysia, and other parts of Southeast Asia. It consists of poached chicken served atop fragrant rice cooked in chicken broth, accompanied by a variety of condiments. The chicken is typically tender and succulent, with a silky-smooth texture and the rice is cooked in the same flavorful broth. The dish is traditionally served with three distinct sauces: a tangy chili sauce, a pungent ginger paste, and a dark soy sauce.




Address:

Boon Tong Kee (Balestier Road)

403 Balestier Road, Singapore, 329801

Ponggol Nasi Lemak

Ponggol Nasi Lemak is a popular variation of the traditional Malaysian and Singaporean dish, nasi lemak. Originating from Punggol, a town in Singapore, this version of nasi lemak features fragrant coconut rice served with a variety of accompaniments.
The combination of savory, spicy, and aromatic flavors makes Ponggol Nasi Lemak a beloved comfort food and a popular choice among locals and visitors alike, seeking a satisfying and hearty meal in Singapore.





Address:

Ponggol Nasi Lemak

371 Jalan Besar, #01-01, Singapore, 208998

Chilli Crab

These Chilli Crabs is a renowned dish served at popular seafood restaurant in Singapore. This dish features fresh, succulent crab cooked in a rich and flavorful chili-based sauce. The crabs are typically stir-fried with a blend of aromatic spices, including garlic, ginger, and shallots, along with chili paste or fresh chili peppers for a spicy kick. The Chilli Crabs are best enjoyed with steamed or fried mantou (Chinese buns) to soak up the delicious sauce. This dish is beloved for its bold and robust flavors, making it a must-try for seafood enthusiasts and visitors looking to experience the vibrant culinary scene of Singapore.

Address:

Mellben Seafood

232 Ang Mo Kio Ave 3, #01-1222 St 22, Singapore 560232
